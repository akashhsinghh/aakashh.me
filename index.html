2026 · Tech Sarthi internship

Posture Expert

AI Tennis Shot Analyzer

Upload a photo or a video of a shot. MediaPipe finds the body's landmarks, the engine measures ten joint angles — knees, arm lift, elbows, wrists, torso lean — and compares each against ideal ranges that change with the shot type and the player's handedness. Out comes a form score, a per-category breakdown, and a ranked list of exactly what to fix, in degrees.
